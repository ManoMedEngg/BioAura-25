About College

Kings Engineering College focuses on industry-relevant technical education, research, and innovation to uplift society. We prioritize high-quality teaching, career guidance, and supporting economically disadvantaged students.

Committed to excellence, we are approved by AICTE and affiliated with Anna University since 2001.

About the Department

The Undergraduate Programme B.E. Biomedical Engineering started in the year 2019 and is functioning effectively to impart quality education.

The department has highly qualified and well experienced faculties in areas like Instrumentation, Biomedical Engineering, Medical electronics, and more. They are actively involved in the Teaching–Learning Process and Research activities.

Theme: Present innovative ideas on futuristic medical technologies.

Topics: AI & Digital Health, Biomedical Devices, Biotechnology, Advanced Materials, and more.

Rules: Paper Presentation Teams of 1-3 members must submit a 250-300 word abstract before the deadline( will be anownce later). Participants will then present their work using a PPT of 8-12 slides, with 5 minutes allocated for the presentation and 3 minutes for a Q&A session. Judging will focus on the project's relevance and innovation, the clarity of the presentation, and how effectively the team handles questions from the panel.
Poster Presentation Teams of up to 3 members are required to design a standard A1 size poster that clearly outlines their project's objectives, methodology, and conclusions. Each team will have 5 minutes to present their poster, followed by a 2-minute Q&A. Posters must be visually appealing and readable from a distance, with judging criteria based on creativity, technical content, relevance to the theme, and the presenters' explanation skills.
